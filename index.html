Privacy Information

Local Data Storage

This application stores your data locally in your browser to enhance your experience:

  • Text content: Your writing is automatically saved as you type.
  • Theme preference: Your chosen color theme persists across sessions.
  • Font preference: Your selected font is remembered.


Privacy Protection

  • Local only: All data stays on your device - nothing is sent to external servers.
  • No tracking: We never track your usage or collect analytics.
  • Your control: You can clear this data anytime through your browser settings or the button below.


Shared Device Warning

If using a shared computer, other users may be able to see your stored content by opening this page!

Consider using private/incognito mode or manually clearing data when finished.
